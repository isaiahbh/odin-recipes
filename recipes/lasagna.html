Ingredients

  • Meat: This lasagna utilizes sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion & Garlic: One onion & two cloves of garlic.
  • Tomatoes: One can crushed tomatoes, two cans tomato sauce, & two cans tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of sugar.
  • Spices & Seasonings: Pinch of fresh Parsley, dried basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, & pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Homemade or store-bought.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, Mozzarella, & Ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: One egg to bind the Ricotta cheese.
